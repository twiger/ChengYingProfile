Introduction

EAS provides more insight into the reason why a student get the grade. Instead of only assessing the students by the grade, teachers can provide the questions of the exam and the answers wrote by students to EAS. EAS will provide several charts and tables, shown in the following demo section, for teachers to understand the problem faced by students and assess the effectiveness of the exams.

My Responsibility

Being a requirement engineer to elicit the requirement from teachers and verify the software specification through paper prototyping. Being a back-end developer to coordinate with a front-end developer and construct the platform with TurboGears. Being a decision maker to terminate the project based on the domain analysis result.
