Project - HW Review Tool

計概批改程式作業工具

Introduction

As an IntroCS assistant, verifying the programming homework submitted by students is its duty. However, students have to complete five kinds of programming homework one semester five times, and there are fifty students in a class. How to verify such numerous programs accurately becomes a serious issue faced by assistants. HW Review Tool help assistant automatically execute the submitted programs according to predefined test cases and generate the reports of each test cases. Besides, the tool also prepares an excel for assistants to open these reports, review the reports, and give the grade with comments to students. The burden afforded by assistants is mitigated.

My Responsibility

Maintaining, modifying, and using the tool to review more than 15 kinds of homework for two years. Evolving the tool to generate the excel for opening the reports smoothly, giving the grade conveniently, and analyzing the document described the submitted programs with comments automatically.
